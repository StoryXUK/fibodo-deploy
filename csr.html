Corporate Social Responsibility

At fibodo, we believe technology should do more than drive business — it should create positive change. That’s why we use our platform to empower communities, support wellbeing, and give back to good causes.

Supporting NHS staff and their families.

The Everything App

Wellbeing for Frontline Workers

Our flagship initiative, The Everything App, provides NHS staff and their families with free access to fitness, Coach, and wellbeing resources. From live-streamed classes and on-demand workouts to discounts on local services, it’s designed to support those who give so much to their communities.

Giving Back

Donating Our Tech to Good Causes

Many charities and community projects don’t have the resources to access modern tech. That’s why fibodo donates booking systems, video tools, and expertise to initiatives that align with our mission. By removing barriers, we help great causes focus on what matters most — their impact.

  • Free platforms for charities
  • Streaming tools for community projects
  • Expertise donated to social initiatives

Technology with purpose.
