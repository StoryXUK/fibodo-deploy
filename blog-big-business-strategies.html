Subscriptions: Turn One-Time Clients into Regulars

Imagine having a steady stream of income you can count on each month. That’s what subscriptions (or memberships) do. Instead of hoping clients rebook, you offer them an easy way to commit to your services with a monthly plan.

  • Personal Trainers: Create a membership that bundles weekly sessions with access to your online workout videos — it’s like Netflix for fitness.
  • Beauty Salons: Launch a “Glow Club” where clients pay monthly for a treatment and get exclusive discounts on extras.
  • Golf Pros: Offer seasonal memberships that include lessons, range time, and equipment rentals.

For digital creators, subscriptions are a goldmine. Monetise online workouts, tutorials, or coaching programs to turn your passion into predictable revenue.

Vouchers: Gifts That Grow Your Business

Vouchers aren’t just holiday stocking stuffers—they’re a year-round way to attract new clients and delight regulars. Plus, they’re a shareable way for your customers to spread the word.

  • A beauty salon sells “pamper packs” as gift vouchers so recipients can choose their favourite treatment.
  • A personal trainer offers a discounted “5-session starter pack” — perfect for new clients.
  • A golf pro sells lesson vouchers that can be gifted or used as prizes at club events.
Dynamic Pricing: Work Smarter, Not Harder

Airlines and hotels tweak prices based on demand — you can, too. Use dynamic pricing to fill your schedule and boost income.

  • Off-peak deals: Discount quiet slots (e.g., Tuesdays) to keep chairs and classes full.
  • Peak premiums: Charge a little more for early mornings and evenings when demand is highest.
  • Seasonal specials: Run budget-friendly clinics in winter to keep clients engaged year-round.
