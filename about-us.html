About fibodo

fibodo is the platform for service — built to empower the coaches, trainers, and business owners who help people live healthier, happier lives. We’re creating the operating system for the experience economy: one place to manage bookings, take payments, connect with clients, and grow your business without the chaos of disconnected tools.

Our belief is simple, technology should give time back, not take it away. That’s why fibodo takes care of the admin, so you can focus on what truly matters: helping people move, learn, and feel their best.

Why we exist

Across the fitness, wellness and service industries, millions of independent professionals and businesses are still juggling spreadsheets, message threads and payment links just to run their day. And video streaming and hosting services come with more cost, complications and admin. That fragmentation limits their income, drains their energy, and holds communities back from accessing the expertise that changes lives.

fibodo changes that. We built a platform that unites everything, booking, payments, client management, marketing, into one intelligent, intuitive hub. Whether you’re coaching one-to-one, running a class, managing multiple locations, or streaming content across the globe fibodo, helps you save time, grow faster, and deliver a better experience for every client.

Trusted by local businesses & global brands

Proven Impact

fibodo has been building the digital backbone of the experience economy for more than a decade. Born from the energy of the London 2012 Olympics, we set out to make it easier for people to discover and book local activities — and to help professionals turn their skills into sustainable businesses that strengthen communities. An early beta with The Telegraph in 2013 proved the model, followed by powering National Golf Month from 2015 to 2017, helping clubs across the UK attract thousands of new players and boost local participation. By 2018, fibodo was supporting corporate wellbeing programmes for global brands including Sodexo, Diageo, Shell, Jaguar Land Rover, and Johnson & Johnson, expanding our reach from community sport to enterprise wellness. In 2021, we achieved a major milestone — processing over 40,000 bookings each month across fitness, sport, and coaching businesses. And in 2025, our partnership with DoingOurBit (DoB) and NHS Trusts led to the launch of The Everything App, giving healthcare staff and families free access to live and on-demand wellbeing content. Beyond technology, fibodo is driving real-world outcomes — keeping the community pound local, creating job opportunities in health and education, and embedding environmentally responsible innovation that reduces waste, travel, and operational impact. Each milestone reinforces our mission: when you make it easier for people to deliver what they love, everyone benefits — economically, socially, and sustainably.
